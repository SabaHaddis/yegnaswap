Why We Built YegnaSwap: A Community-Driven Marketplace for Everyone

YegnaSwap was born from the vision of university students in Addis Ababa who wanted to create a platform that empowers individuals to share their possessions and talents. This community-first tool allows users to resell items, promote their handmade creations, and offer various services, fostering a vibrant local marketplace. By connecting people, YegnaSwap helps strengthen community ties while supporting local entrepreneurship.
